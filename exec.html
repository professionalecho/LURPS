Treasurer

{{ page.treasurer }} is in charge of the society’s money. They take annual membership fees and keep track of how the money is being spent.

President

{{ page.president }} is the leader of the society. They are the representative of the society to outsiders, and chair all the meetings.

Secretary

{{ page.secretary }} is the primary administrator of the society. They take minutes at every meeting and act as returning officer at general meetings.

Social Secretary

{{ page.socialsec }} organises non-roleplay-based social events for members. They are responsible for organising almost everything the society does outside its normal operating hours.

Tabletop Rep

{{ page.tabletoprep }} is responsible for organising the tabletop games at LURPS. They book rooms for games and oversee proceedings at pitch days.

Live Rep

{{ page.liverep }} is responsible for all LARP events in the society. They ensure games go ahead every week, and that the safety codes of LUSU are upheld at all times.

Webmaster

{{ page.webmaster }} maintains the online presence of LURPS. They keep this very website up to scratch, as well as running the social media of the society.
